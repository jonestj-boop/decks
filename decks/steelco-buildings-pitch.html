On-Page Issues (All 3 Pages)

  • Duplicate H2 structure — all 3 pages use identical H2s: “The SCB Difference,” “What Our Clients Have to Say,” “Industry Stats,” “FAQs”
  • H2 duplicates H1 on Self-Storage and Commercial pages

  • ~16 images missing ALT text per page
  • Generic OG:title shared across all 3 pages — kills social sharing
  • No FAQ schema despite FAQ sections on every page
  • No Product/Service schema — no structured data for rich results

Content Differentiation Problem

The Warehouses page has unique use-case content (e-commerce fulfillment, cold-chain storage, spec space). This is why it previously ranked well.

Self-Storage and Commercial pages are essentially the same template with swapped keywords. Google sees this as thin/duplicate content and deprioritizes accordingly.

Fix: Each product page needs 2,500-3,500 words of unique, intent-matched content — use cases, cost breakdowns, sizing guides, comparison tables, and buyer journey content specific to that building type.
