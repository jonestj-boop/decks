Why This Plan

How we picked these 120 keywords — and what we deliberately left out.

Every keyword scored on three things: monthly search volume, keyword difficulty, and brand fit. We picked the 120 that win on all three. The rest are documented in the workbook so you can see what we considered and why we cut it.

The four pillars

Joint & Mobility · 32 articles

Glucosamine, MSM, hip and joint, senior mobility, arthritis support. Highest single-keyword volume in your category — anchor pillar.

Calming & Anxiety · 32 articles

Calming chews, separation anxiety, storm and travel calmers, CBD comparisons. High emotional intent — exceptional conversion rates.

Gut Health & Digestion · 28 articles

Probiotics, prebiotics, digestive enzymes, sensitive stomach support. Fastest-growing search category in pet supplements (40% YoY).

Skin, Coat & Allergies · 28 articles

Allergy chews, omega-3, fish oil, skin and coat care. Strong cross-sell into joint and gut categories — same buyers, different intent.

What we deliberately excluded

  • ×Veterinary diagnosis content — VCA, AKC, PetMD own these SERPs and they carry medical-claim liability for a supplement brand
