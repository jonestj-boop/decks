Why This Plan

Five pillars chosen on purpose. Two competitor categories deliberately ignored.

The keyword universe has 4,897 candidates. We selected 120 by mapping them against Retreat's modalities, scoring for capturable difficulty, and excluding three categories on principle.

Stress & Anxiety Relief 30

Highest-volume pillar. Anxiety-relief queries are top-of-mind for buyers and feed the strongest commercial intent. Powers brand discovery.

Guided Meditation 28

Category default — but Calm and Headspace own the broad terms. We win the modality-specific subset (10-min, sleep, beginner, IFS-flavored guides).

IFS & Emotional Healing 25

Retreat's true differentiator. Calm and Headspace barely cover IFS, EMDR, or trauma-informed modalities. Wide-open territory.

Mindfulness Practices 22

Educational base layer. Covers benefits, principles, science. Fast-indexing, evergreen, and the favorite citation source for AI engines.

Somatic Breathwork 15

Niche but high-conversion. Body-based modalities are growing fast in the wellness category. Lowest competition pillar in the plan.

What we deliberately excluded

  • Calm/Headspace branded queries — we don't write articles to rank for "calm app" or "headspace login". Wrong intent, wrong audience.
  • Sleep stories / sleep meditation broad terms — Calm dominates this with 200+ hours of audio inventory. Not where we win in year 1.
  • Religious / spiritual-tradition specific content — IFS and somatic work crosses traditions; we stay modality-focused for brand consistency.
  • Pure clinical / medical topics — disclaimer-heavy, slow to publish, low brand fit. Therapy modalities only when contextual to the practice.
