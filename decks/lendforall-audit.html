Technical Audit · Critical

The two fixes that come first

Critical  Privacy Policy → HTTP 403

lendforall.ca/privacy-policy returns 403 Forbidden to every visitor. The footer links to it from all 243 crawled pages, so the whole site funnels internal-link equity into an error page.

Fix: likely a security-plugin or server rule blocking the URL. Restore the page (or repoint the footer link), then confirm 200 status in GSC URL Inspection. ~1 hour of dev time.

Critical  Toronto page canonical bug

/bad-credit-loans-toronto is live and marked index, follow — but its canonical tag points at the homepage. Google obeys the canonical and drops the page from rankings for Toronto queries (the single largest loan-search market in Canada).

Fix: set the canonical to the page's own URL (one field in the SEO plugin), then request reindexing. ~15 minutes.

High  13 noindexed blog posts

Posts including /how-to-apply-for-a-10000-loan-in-canada, /tips-for-getting-approved-for-a-loan-with-bad-credit, and /the-right-choice-installment-vs-payday-loans carry noindex, follow tags. If these were deliberately pruned, no action needed — but several target commercial queries and could be refreshed and reindexed instead of hidden.

Medium  /private-mortgage-lenders-canada is a 404 that still receives internal links — remove the link or 301 it to a relevant live page (an inbound icash.ca backlink also passes through a 301 at /installment-loan-canada/).
