Technical Audit — Critical Findings

Two Fixes That Come First

Both verified manually, both actively costing rankings, both fixable inside a week.

lendforall.ca/privacy-policy
CRITICAL — HTTP 403 FORBIDDEN
The privacy policy is blocked for every visitor and every crawler — and the footer links to it from all 243 pages. For a lending site (a "Your Money or Your Life" category in Google's guidelines), a dead privacy policy undermines the exact trust signals Google weighs hardest. Fix: unblock the URL (likely a security-plugin rule) — ~1 hour.
lendforall.ca/bad-credit-loans-toronto
CRITICAL — CANONICAL POINTS TO HOMEPAGE
The page is live and marked index, follow — but its canonical tag declares it a duplicate of the homepage, so Google drops it from Toronto loan searches, the largest loan market in Canada. Fix: one field in the SEO plugin + reindex request — ~15 minutes.
13 blog posts — incl. /how-to-apply-for-a-10000-loan-in-canada
HIGH — NOINDEXED CONTENT
Thirteen posts carry noindex tags, several targeting commercial queries (installment vs payday, bad-credit approval tips). If deliberate pruning, fine — but the strongest should be refreshed and reindexed, not hidden.
lendforall.ca/private-mortgage-lenders-canada
MEDIUM — 404 WITH LIVE INTERNAL LINKS
A deleted page still receives internal links; an inbound icash.ca backlink also passes through a 301 redirect. Both leak equity — redirect and reclaim.

Title tags over 60 characters Medium 90* *Most exceed 60 only because of the 22-character " - Lend For All Canada" suffix Yoast appends to every title site-wide: Google truncates the brand name, not the keyword, so these are cosmetic.
