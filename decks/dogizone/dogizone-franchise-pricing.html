Pricing Philosophy

How Pricing Works in the DogiZone Franchise System

Fair Market Alignment

Franchisee pricing matches standard market retail rates — no inflation, no hidden costs. You pay what any business would pay for the same level of service.

Centralized Efficiency

Centralized execution creates cost efficiencies at scale. Shared systems mean better performance and lower ramp time for new locations entering the network.

Volume Benefits, Not Markups

HQ benefits from volume economies, not franchisee markups. As the network grows, operational efficiency increases — but your pricing stays consistent.

Long-Term Protection

This model protects brand consistency, marketing results, and long-term growth for every location in the system.
