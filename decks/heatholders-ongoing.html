Root Cause Analysis

Why Rankings Decay Without Maintenance

Three compounding factors explain the decline — all of them fixable.

✍️

Publishing Gaps

Google rewards consistent publishing signals. Long pauses between batches allow competitor content to close the gap.

  • 6-month gap Apr–Sep 2024
  • 4-month gap Mar–Jul 2025
  • No publishing Nov 2025 onward
  • Freshness signals decay without new content
Result: Blog keyword rankings dropped from 33,962 → 18,494 after the last publishing pause.
🔗

Link Profile Deteriorating

29,424 total backlinks sounds strong — but only 2.3% carry follow authority, and 385 vital links have already been lost.

  • Only 679 of 29,424 links are "Best" follow links
  • 385 Lost & Vital links need recovery
  • Top backlinks are low-quality directory spam
  • Priority collection pages lack editorial links
See next slide for the full link profile breakdown and impact map.
🔄

Content Aging

Articles published in 2023–2024 are now 1–2 years old. Without updates, Google progressively de-ranks them in favor of fresher competitors.

  • Top performers showing declining CTR
  • Seasonal articles need annual refreshes
  • Internal linking gaps between old and new posts
  • Schema markup not maximized on high-traffic pages
Fix: A content refresh cadence on the top 20 posts by traffic can recapture lost impressions quickly.
