Section 3

The Strategy

Three pillars driving the consolidation — every decision maps back to more traffic, more leads, and lower costs.

🔍

SEO & Domain Authority

  • 301 redirect every myarkansasprobate.com URL to its new home — zero link equity lost
  • Consolidate overlapping probate content from both blogs into authoritative, deduplicated articles
  • Unified schema markup (LocalBusiness + ProfessionalService) for richer search results
  • One domain building authority across all keywords instead of two domains splitting it
📈

Lead Generation & Conversions

  • Single form system with unified submissions — every lead in one pipeline
  • Full-funnel tracking: GTM, GA4, Google Ads, FB Pixel across all pages
  • Probate visitors now enter the property buyer funnel seamlessly
  • Attorney referral network drives B2B leads to the same brand
💰

Cost Savings & Simplicity

  • Eliminate Carrot platform fees — move to standard WordPress hosting
  • One hosting bill, one theme, one set of plugins to maintain
  • Single analytics dashboard instead of checking two separate properties
  • Content team updates one site, not two — faster execution on everything
