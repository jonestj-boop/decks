Three Pillars to Dominate Your 10-Mile Radius

No paid ads. No shortcuts. Just the three levers that move Google Maps rankings for local home service businesses.

📍

Google Business Profile

Your GBP is the single highest-leverage ranking factor for Maps. Most businesses leave 60% of its power unused.

  • Complete all 40+ GBP attributes for garage doors
  • Add all services with keyword-rich descriptions
  • Publish 4 posts/month (offers, tips, jobs)
  • Q&A seeding for high-intent queries
  • Photo strategy — before/after installs monthly
  • Set service area radius correctly for 10 miles
🌐

Local Landing Pages

Google ranks pages, not websites. One page optimized for "garage door repair Greensboro" can drive calls all on its own.

  • 6–10 city-specific service pages (Greensboro, Summerfield, Kernersville, Stokesdale, McLeansville, Oak Ridge)
  • Each page targets 3–5 local keywords
  • Local schema markup (LocalBusiness)
  • Embedded Google Maps per page
  • Neighborhood-specific trust signals
  • Clear CTAs tied to phone number
⭐

Reviews & Citations

Reviews are a top-3 Maps ranking factor. Citations tell Google your business is real. Both are systematized — not luck.

  • Review request system (text + email after every job)
  • Templates leveraging your military/first responder offers
  • Target 10+ new reviews/month
  • 50 core directory citations (Yelp, Angi, BBB, HomeAdvisor)
  • NAP audit + corrections across existing listings
  • Competitor review gap monitoring
