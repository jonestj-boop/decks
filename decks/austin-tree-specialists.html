Audit Findings — Technical Local SEO

What We Found Under the Hood

Four fixable gaps that suppress local rankings, verified directly on austintreespecialists.com in July 2026.

Finding Severity Detail Why It Matters
Missing LocalBusiness schema High Only WebSite + Organization JSON-LD present. No LocalBusiness/TreeService type, no service catalog, no areaServed, no review markup despite 174 reviews. Google and AI answer engines rely on structured data to connect your site to "tree service near me" queries.
Address consistency risk Medium Schema lists "5900 Balcones Drive #19178" — a suite-number format that should be verified against the Google Business Profile and citation network for exact NAP match. Inconsistent name/address/phone across directories dilutes Maps ranking trust.
No FAQ or service schema Medium An FAQ page exists but carries no FAQPage markup; 7 service pages carry no Service markup. Forfeits rich results and the exact Q&A format AI Overviews quote from.
Undated, generic blog Medium 20 posts, no visible publish dates, interchangeable "how to spot…" titles with heavy topic overlap and no seasonal or local hooks. Freshness signals are invisible; overlapping posts compete with each other instead of building topical authority.
Good news: every one of these is a build-once fix or a content-process fix — none requires replatforming the site.
