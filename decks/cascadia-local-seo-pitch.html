Internal Linking Model

Every local blog should strengthen a local pillar.

Each Blog Links To
  • The relevant city hub
  • The matching city pillar
  • The relevant national service page
  • The relevant national industry page
Each Pillar Links To
  • The city hub
  • 3–5 supporting blogs
  • Quote / contact CTA
  • Adjacent local pillars

This turns the blog from a traffic channel into an authority channel.
