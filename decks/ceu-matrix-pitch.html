CASR's Exploitable Weaknesses

No blog or informational content. Their site is entirely certification requirement pages and course pages — zero "how to" guides, career explainers, or salary articles. That entire content tier is wide open for CEU Matrix to own.
No criminal justice content. CEU Matrix is CJCA-aligned and serves the criminal justice counselor audience. CASR has zero CJ content — that's a differentiated audience segment you own and they don't target.
Heavy table structure = thin keyword density. Their pages are mostly requirement tables, not narrative content. Pages with high table-to-text ratios often underperform on semantic relevance signals. Deeper, more narrative pages from CEU Matrix can rank above them with less content volume.
