Where we point it first

Cast lures across fast-moving verticals, then double down

Private security and contractors

VP Compliance / Operations. High turnover means constant retraining, and budgets that move far faster than government.

Healthcare L&D

VP Training / Safety. An early Mass General nursing study points to reduced patient falls, a clean, quantifiable risk-and-liability story.

Corporate and regulated L&D

VP Training / Compliance. Mandatory certs decay after test day; a readiness dashboard proves teams stay sharp. Faster budgets, plus med-device rep enablement.

How we keep it disciplined

  • Run the same risk-reduction offer into 2-3 verticals at once.
  • Measure reply and meeting rates by segment, message and title.
  • Cut what's quiet, pour budget into whoever responds, then expand.
  • Law enforcement and first responders sit in a secondary tier (slower procurement); revisit once a faster vertical proves out.
Defense and aviation stay the core

They keep feeding inbound on their own. These commercial tests are the new ground outbound is built to win.
