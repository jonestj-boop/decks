The Strategy

The Pillar-Cluster Model

Each Pillar page targets a broad, high-volume keyword. Cluster pages target related long-tail variants and all link back to the pillar — telling Google that Champ3D is the authority on that topic.

🏛️

Pillar Pages (4 Total)

  • 1,500–3,000 word comprehensive service guides
  • Targets a broad, high-volume head keyword
  • Lives at /services/[topic] or /resources/[topic]
  • Receives internal links from all related cluster posts
  • Primary landing pages for both organic and paid traffic
📝

Cluster Content (25–40/month)

  • 800–1,200 word targeted articles and guides
  • Captures specific long-tail questions and buyer intent searches
  • Each post links back to its parent Pillar page
  • Published at consistent velocity to build topical momentum
  • Covers comparisons, how-tos, use cases, and FAQ content
Why it works on a new site: Google rewards topical authority. Publishing 25+ tightly linked pieces around one topic signals "this site is the expert" — accelerating rankings across the entire cluster, not just individual pages.
