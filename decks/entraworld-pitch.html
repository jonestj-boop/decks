Section 2

Five Audiences, Five Campaigns

EntraWorld's market is massive, but the proof of concept requires focused targeting. Each ICP gets its own landing page, messaging, and ad creative.

🎓

1. Students & Young Founders

Young people ready to skip the 4-year degree and build a business instead. The current climate is perfect: resourcefulness over credentials. Messaging: "Your MBA in your pocket."

💼

2. Struggling Professionals

Doctors, lawyers, and skilled professionals who know their craft but don't know business. They've never written a business plan or made a cold call. Messaging: "You don't have to be a business expert. You just have to be resourceful."

🤝

3. Business Partners

Insurance brokers, patent attorneys, POS providers, and service companies who want to integrate with and resell through the EntraWorld marketplace. Gain-sharing and embedded partnerships.

💰

4. Investors

Angel investors and high-net-worth individuals looking for proof of concept. The pitch: $50M valuation at 75K users. Break-even by month 3. One slide: "Look what we got out of this spend."

🏫

5. Schools & Education Institutions

Starting with Babson College. EntraWorld as a virtual preparatory school for entrepreneurship. White-label university content for junior high and high school students. EntraStart as the youth-focused subsidiary, with potential to replicate across every school, every country.

🎯
Campaign architecture: Five personas, five landing pages, five ad creative sets. The content engine (40 articles/month) maps directly to these audiences. Paid campaigns run separate funnels per persona so we can measure which audience converts fastest and redirect budget accordingly in month 3.

Section 4

Services & Phased Rollout

Four workstreams that run in parallel. One-time setup costs (site build, GHL) are spread across M1-M2, with M3 shifting budget toward optimization and scaling what works.

🌐

Website Redesign: Webflow

  • Full design and build on Webflow (10 core pages + blog CMS)
  • Responsive across all devices: desktop, tablet, mobile
  • Teal + gold brand system with 2-font typography stack
  • 5 persona-specific landing pages (one per ICP)
  • Lead capture forms on every key page (connected to GoHighLevel)
  • GA4 + GTM setup with event tracking (signup, upgrade, engagement)
  • Schema markup: Organization, WebSite, FAQPage
  • Webflow CMS for blog with clean URL structure, categories, author system
🔍

SEO + Content Engine

  • Technical SEO audit and full implementation (on the new build)
  • Full-funnel keyword research, mapped to pages and content plan
  • 40 articles/month via ClusterMagic, publish-ready, mapped to all 5 ICPs
  • Topic clusters: entrepreneurship, startup tools, funding, validation, youth programs
  • Internal linking strategy across all content
  • Quarterly keyword refresh and content gap analysis
📣

Paid Media: Google + Meta + LinkedIn

  • Campaign architecture per ICP: 5 audiences, 5 funnels
  • Google + Meta for broad acquisition, LinkedIn for investor/partner/professional targeting
  • Creative angles: interview-style, product walkthrough, problem-solution, vs. ads, UGC
  • AI creative production (Kling, Seedance, Minimax, Sora). One concept, 10 variations
  • College/campus-specific campaigns as needed (geofencing, radius targeting)
  • Retargeting for freemium-to-paid push
  • Event tracking: freemium signup (primary), engagement depth (secondary), upgrade (retarget)
  • Weekly experimentation: creative, audience, bid, and landing page testing
  • If one ICP outperforms, redirect budget into that audience in M3
⚡

GoHighLevel Setup & Automation

  • Full GHL account setup and configuration
  • CRM pipelines: Freemium → Engaged → Trial → Paid
  • Lead capture forms integrated with Webflow site
  • Email onboarding sequence (5-7 emails for new freemium signups)
  • Freemium-to-paid nurture sequence + SMS re-engagement
  • SalesCloser AI + WebinarKit + Twilio integration support
  • Dashboard and reporting for pipeline visibility
