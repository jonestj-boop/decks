Why This Plan

Five pillars chosen on purpose. Three categories deliberately excluded.

The keyword universe surfaced 2,417 candidates from SEMrush Strategy Builder. We selected 120 by mapping them against your toolkit product line, scoring for capturable difficulty, and excluding categories that don't serve the brand or the buyer.

Compliance Software & Tools 32

Anchor pillar. Broad commercial-intent keywords — "compliance software," "compliance management tools," "compliance documentation." The category-level content that captures buyers before they've decided which standard they need.

ISO 9001 / Quality Management 25

Quality Management System (QMS). Largest single-standard keyword pool — ISO 9001 has the broadest market because it applies across every industry. Anchor keyword has KD 79 but adjacent ones ("iso 9001 checklist," "iso 9001 implementation") are KD 20-40.

ISO 27001 / Information Security 22

Information Security. Your buyer's #1 stated focus from onboarding ("rank for compliance toolkits") and your highest-cannibalization keyword. Consolidation in M1 + entity-rich hub in M2 makes this the fastest revenue-driving pillar.

Audit Preparation & Certification 21

Audit Preparation & Certification. The execution layer — checklists, audit prep guides, certification process explainers. Highest concentration of low-KD easy-win keywords in the universe. Indexes fastest, drives newsletter and email captures.

SOC 2 / Security Audit 20

SOC 2 / Security Audit. Fastest-growing intent in the data — SaaS companies preparing for SOC 2 are searching aggressively. Less crowded than ISO standards because SOC 2 is newer and Advisera/ISMS focus more on ISO.

What we deliberately excluded

  • Competitor-branded queries — "advisera login," "isms.online pricing," etc. Wrong audience intent and impossible to outrank a brand for its own name.
  • Industry-specific compliance (HIPAA, PCI-DSS, FedRAMP) — adjacent to your product line but each has its own dedicated specialist competitor and regulatory complexity. Stays out unless you expand the toolkit catalog.
  • Free template / "free download" queries — high search volume but the buyer expects free content. Cannibalizes the paid-toolkit value proposition.
  • Auditor / consultant career content — "how to become an iso 27001 auditor" type queries. Wrong audience — you sell to the people hiring auditors, not the auditors themselves.

What you should expect

  1. Month 1 — Cannibalization fixes ship. First 40 articles publish. Indexing visible week 3. First page-1 wins on consolidated pages by week 6.
  2. Month 3 — All 120 published. Schema rolled out site-wide. ~1,000 monthly organic. First AI Mode citation pages start to appear.
  3. Month 6 — Hub pages clustering, AI Visibility score climbing past 25. ~5,000 monthly organic. Several core toolkit keywords on page 1.
  4. Month 12 — Compounding kicks in across pillars. ~15,000 monthly organic. AI Visibility 35+ — approaching Advisera-mid-tier presence in AI answers.
How each article is built: entity-rich brief → AI-assisted draft → expert editorial pass → schema + internal linking → publish as draft to Shopify for review. Same pipeline that scaled the case-study client to 500K monthly.
