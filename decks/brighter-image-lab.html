Paid Media — Key Findings

Four Issues Costing You Right Now

Each finding is an independent lever. Fix all four and the same ad budget produces materially better returns.

01 — Search Demand Trapped in PMax
99% of your $140K Brand campaign runs on Google Search — but inside a Performance Max wrapper that removes all query-level control. You're spending Search dollars with none of Search's visibility or control.
02 — Competitors Campaign Leakage
$22,132 went to YouTube at 1.16x and $2,332 to Discover where 30 conversions are worth $30 total. Only 39% of the $41K Competitors budget actually reaches Search — the channel that works.
03 — Budget Inverted Relative to Efficiency
Press On Veneers runs at 8.74x ROAS and gets $1,266. The Competitors campaign runs at 1.02x and gets $41,254. Your best performer is starved; your breakeven campaign is fully funded.
04 — Live Throttling Active Right Now
Most asset groups on both live PMax campaigns are limited by policy — reducing reach and quality. The Brand campaign is also "limited by budget" — a cap on your highest-volume converter. These are correctable today.
What's working: Brand Search core is healthy at 2.60x. Press On Veneers at 8.74x proves the model. The account just needs better structure and budget allocation to match.
