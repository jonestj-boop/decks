Audit Findings

Why You're Not in the Map Pack

Google's local ranking algorithm comes down to three pillars: Relevance, Proximity, and Prominence. Here's where the gaps are.

GBP & Local Signals

📋
Incomplete Google Business Profile
Missing service attributes, Q&A, photo uploads, and business categories — GBP completeness is the #1 Map Pack ranking factor.
💬
Low review velocity
Google rewards recency AND volume. Competitors actively generate new reviews weekly. Review count is the #2 local ranking signal.
📸
Inactive GBP posting
No regular posts, new photos, or offers on GBP. Active profiles signal trust and recency to Google's algorithm.
📍
Citation inconsistencies
NAP (Name, Address, Phone) mismatches across directories confuse Google and suppress local rankings.

Technical & Content

🔧
No Local Business Schema markup
Schema tells Google exactly what your business is and where. Without it, you're invisible to structured data indexing.
📝
Thin content + footer keyword stuffing
Pages lack depth; footer stuffed with repetitive limo variations. Google penalizes both — it reads as low-quality.
🔗
Weak backlink profile
~45 referring domains vs. May's 280+. Low domain authority caps how far you can rank, even with good on-page SEO.
🗺️
No Google Map embed on site
Embedding your GMB map on key pages reinforces geo-relevance signals — a quick win that's currently missing.
