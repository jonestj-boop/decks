Workstream 3

Page Architecture: Match Pages to Searches

Two structural plays — both driven by search volume, both improving customer experience as much as SEO.

🚚

Vehicle-Specific Product Pages

  • Today: one rock light page serves truck, Jeep, and ATV/UTV shoppers — the breadcrumb changes, the page doesn't.
  • The play: split popular multi-vehicle products into dedicated pages — e.g. Rock Lights for Trucks, Rock Lights for Jeeps, Rock Lights for ATV/UTV.
  • The existing page (with its accumulated authority) becomes the highest-search-volume variant; new pages cover the rest.
  • Copy, images, and reviews all become vehicle-specific — "rock lights for trucks" searches land on a truck page.
  • Only where volume justifies it: no rock lights page for cars, no underglow page for ATVs.
  • Builds on the Phase 1 / Phase 2 prioritization sheet your team already started (shared via access@embertribe.com).
💡

"Shop by Light Type" Category Pages

  • Today: the site filters by vehicle only. Competitors rank their light-type category pages for searches like "rock lights" and "underglow lights."
  • The play: build category pages by light type — light bars, light pods, rock lights, whips, headlights — where XKGlow has 3–6 products each.
  • EmberTribe delivers a category list ranked by search volume and keyword difficulty for your approval before anything is built.
  • Add a "Shop by Light Type" entry to top navigation; retire low-traffic vehicle entries from the menu without deleting the pages.
  • Captures product-first searchers who don't start from their vehicle.

Workstreams 4 & 5

Authority: Backlinks + Reddit Seeding

Rebuilding the authority momentum that stalled when link building stopped 6–7 months ago.

🔗

Quality Backlinks — 2/month

  • Two high-quality placements per month, pointed at the category pages we're trying to rank — not links for links' sake.
  • Priority: newer products and the new light-type category pages as they launch.
  • Domain authority currently 40 (down from 42) — the goal is recovering trajectory, not chasing a vanity number.
  • You previously ran 4/month with your old agency; we start at 2 and scale when results justify it — your call.
🎯

Reddit Seeding — 90-Day Test

  • Brand mentions in the subreddits that already surface in search results for your product terms — no forced links, nothing spammy.
  • You approve everything: we propose the subreddits and the copy; nothing posts without your sign-off.
  • Why it matters: Google and AI assistants (ChatGPT, etc.) license Reddit content — brand mentions there feed both traditional and AI-driven discovery.
  • Structured as a one-quarter test with measured results — if it stops looking organic or doesn't perform, we redirect that budget.
