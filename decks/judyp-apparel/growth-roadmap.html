  • Competitor-branded queries — articles to rank for "anatomie ___" or "jude connally ___" don't convert. Wrong audience intent.

  • Generic style-blogger listicles — articles like "best fashion influencers to follow" don't index well and don't drive product searches. We focus on commercial and informational queries that map to your catalog.
