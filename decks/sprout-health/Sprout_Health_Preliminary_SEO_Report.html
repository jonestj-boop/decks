Executive summary

Sprout Health is investing heavily in paid acquisition in one of the most expensive, most competitive categories online, while organic search remains almost entirely untapped. That is the opportunity. A site with your ad budget and offer should have an organic presence many times its current size. Our plan builds that engine on two tracks: high-velocity, compliant content (400 ClusterMagic articles per month) and authority backlinks (DA30 to DA60), designed to lower your blended cost of acquisition over time and create a channel that does not switch off the moment ad spend pauses.

For context: an established competitor in this space can pull hundreds of thousands of organic visits per month. The gap is the opportunity, not a liability.

The opportunity

  • Organic is underbuilt relative to spend. You are paying premium CPMs for traffic that strong organic content could earn repeatedly at a fraction of the marginal cost.
  • The category rewards depth and trust. Health and weight-loss search is "Your Money or Your Life" content, where Google heavily favors well-sourced, expert-reviewed pages. That is a moat we can build deliberately.
  • The giants own the head terms, not the long tail. Hims, Ro, Found and others dominate broad keywords. The winnable ground is the symptom-led, comparison, and cost questions your future customers actually type before they buy.
  • AI search is in play. Conversational, well-structured content increasingly gets cited in AI Overviews and assistants, a channel most competitors are not optimizing for yet.

The strategy: two engines

Engine 1 — ClusterMagic content (400 articles / month)

  • Topic clusters built around how your buyers actually search, from early symptom questions to high-intent cost and comparison queries.
  • Conversational, evidence-led, and structured for both Google and AI search.
  • Every health article carries a medically-reviewed byline and authoritative citations to meet E-E-A-T standards.
  • Internal linking across the cluster so nothing is orphaned and authority compounds.

Engine 2 — Authority backlinks (DA30–DA60)

  • A monthly mix of links across DA30 to DA60 to lift your domain authority from its current ~22.
  • Costs passed straight through, with a flat management fee instead of a markup, so your budget buys links, not margin.
  • Mix is tuned each month to get the most authority per dollar within budget.

Compliance & trust as a competitive edge

This is where most agencies create risk and where we reduce it. Two items we will address directly:

  • FDA sensitivity. Your category is under active FDA scrutiny around compounded GLP-1 marketing claims. All content is written to avoid non-compliant language (for example, never describing compounded products as "FDA-approved") and is routed to your medical/compliance team for final sign-off before publishing.
  • Reputation in search. Third-party listings, including a BBB profile showing an F rating, currently shape what people find when they search your brand. Building owned, trustworthy content and a stronger branded presence helps reclaim that narrative. We will confirm specifics once we have access.
