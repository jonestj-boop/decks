Why This Plan

Five pillars chosen on purpose. Three categories deliberately excluded.

The keyword universe surfaced 5,243 candidates from SEMrush Strategy Builder. We selected 120 by mapping them against FATCO's product line, scoring for capturable difficulty, and excluding categories that don't serve the brand.

Clean Beauty & Non-Toxic 32

Broadest awareness pillar. The buyer searching 'safe skincare ingredients' or 'non-toxic body care' is upstream of FATCO's product purchase. ToFu content here feeds brand search downstream.

Natural Deodorant 28

FATCO's highest-converting product category by repeat purchase. Strong commercial intent across the keyword set; schema + entity-rich product education unlocks the SERP rich results that drive CTR.

Tallow Education & Sourcing 25

FATCO's defining moat. The brand owns 'real food for your skin' — tallow education, sourcing, and product-specific deep dives are where competitors are weakest and the AI citation pattern is already strongest.

Sensitive Skin Solutions 20

Therapeutic angle. Sensitive-skin buyers are the most loyal segment in personal care — they research deeply and stay once they find a brand that works. High lifetime value.

Natural Personal Care 15

Bath, body, hair adjacents. Lower-volume per keyword but covers the cross-sell footprint that keeps the Article-Plan diversified and the keyword universe defensible.

What we deliberately excluded

  • Competitor-branded queries — articles to rank for "primally pure ___" or "ancestral cosmetics ___" don't convert. Wrong audience intent.
  • Keto / carnivore diet content — adjacent to tallow but a different audience and a regulatory minefield (medical claims). Stays out.
  • DIY tallow recipes / soap-making — high search volume but cannibalizes the brand's commercial intent. We're selling tallow products, not teaching people to render their own.
  • Vague "clean beauty" influencer content — articles like "best clean beauty influencers to follow" don't index well and don't drive product searches.

  4. Month 12 — Compounding kicks in across pillars. ~25,000 monthly organic. AI Visibility 50+ (approaching Established band).
