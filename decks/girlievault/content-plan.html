How to read this plan

Each article targets one primary keyword mapped to a pillar and a funnel stage (BoFu buying guides, MoFu comparison and best-of, ToFu education and styling). Difficulty (KD) is SEMrush's 0-100 score; we front-load easy wins while your domain authority builds. Months are sequenced Foundation, Breadth, then Long-tail.

Leggings & Bottoms Swimwear & Beach Sports Bras & Tops Matching Sets & Outfits Activewear Style & Education Brand, Give-Back & Community

What we deliberately excluded: menswear and couples terms, everyday and lingerie bras (you sell sports bras), off-brand risqué swim, over-50 audience terms, and non-catalog items like socks. The full excluded list lives in the keyword workbook.

Next step: approve the plan and ClusterMagic begins the Month 1 batch. Each article ships entity-rich, internally linked, schema-marked, and posted as a draft to your Shopify for review, with the Reddit and AEO layer running alongside to start earning brand mentions in AI answers.
