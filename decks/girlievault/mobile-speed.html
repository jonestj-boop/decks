Mobile Site Speed · The Fix

Six fixes, sized to your actual page. Most need theme-code and app surgery.

These aren't generic tips. They're ranked by what's actually weighing your store down, straight from the audit.

PriorityFix (and what it's worth on your store)
P1Compress, resize, and lazy-load images. Images are 6.2 MB across 74 files, over half your page weight. The single biggest win.
P1Cut unused JavaScript and defer third-party scripts. ~902 KB of JavaScript goes unused, and third-party apps add 3.2 MB across 101 files (chat, reviews, trackers).
P1Preload the hero image, remove render-blocking CSS/JS. This attacks the 22.6 s load time directly.
P2Audit and remove unused Shopify apps. Fewer apps means fewer of the 384 files loaded on every page.
P2Compress and lazy-load video. Autoplay media adds another 1.2 MB before shoppers scroll.
P3Trim theme code and subset fonts. Remove unused sections, snippets, and font weights that ship on every page.
