Strategy Rationale

Why these pillars, in this order.

Diploma & Transcript Replacement (39 articles)

People searching for diploma replacements are already in intent mode. 39 articles covering every state variation, closed school scenario, and GED edge case — each one linking to the matching collection or product page. This is the fastest path to commercial conversions from organic.

Novelty & Replica Diplomas (27 articles)

Your core commercial content. Buyer guides, comparison posts, use-case articles (film props, gifts, recognition programs) — each directly linked to product pages and embedded with CTAs. These convert. They also attract the long-tail searches that competitors aren’t covering yet.

Degree Types & Education Guide (25 articles)

25,000+ monthly searches from people confused about degree terminology. This content doesn’t sell directly — it builds topical authority and captures top-of-funnel traffic that retargets to commercial pages. Google rewards sites that answer the full topic spectrum.

International + Certs & Specialty (29 articles)

SameDayDiplomas already has collection pages for 15+ countries. These articles support those pages with informational content, improving their rankings. Specialty certificate content expands the commercial surface area beyond diplomas.

What we deliberately excluded

  • Fraud-adjacent content (using fake diplomas for employment) — legal risk, wrong signal to Google
  • Medical/legal credential content — YMYL territory, wrong audience, high KD
  • Keywords with KD > 60 where domain authority isn’t yet positioned to compete
  • Purely local/city-level diploma mill queries — low volume, not scalable
