Appendix · Technical SEO

Priority 1, in detail

🔧 What we found (live HTML audit)

  • root causePages are client-side rendered. Crawlers and AI bots get an empty shell until JavaScript runs.
  • CTR killerEvery page shares one title and has no meta description. /turo-earnings is titled "AI Inbox & Claims Assistant" to people searching for an earnings calculator.
  • missingNo H1, no canonical, no structured data on any page.
  • already strongrobots.txt explicitly welcomes GPTBot & ChatGPT. The AEO groundwork exists, the pages just have nothing for them to read yet.

✅ The fixes

  • 1Unique title + meta description per page (rewrites already drafted).
  • 2Prerender the marketing & tool pages so content exists at crawl time. Unlocks everything else, and is what makes AEO possible.
  • 3Add one H1, canonical tags, and JSON-LD (SoftwareApplication, FAQ).
  • 4Complete OG & Twitter cards for social and LLM previews.
  • 5Refresh sitemap dates and resubmit in Search Console.
