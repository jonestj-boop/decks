Rebuild the Pearland Water Slide Page

Due May 24 ERS

The existing /pearland-water-slide-rentals/ page got 3 impressions in April at position 14. It is effectively invisible. Rebuild it using the League City page (position 7.4, 211 impressions) as the template. Pearland is PKA's home market — it should be the strongest location page on the site and serves as the authority anchor for all nearby city pages.

Steps

  1. 1
    Open the League City water slide page in ERS (/water-slides-rentals-league-city/) and do a full content audit — note the H1, intro paragraph, service area description, product listings, FAQ, and any embedded map or review section. This is the benchmark.
  2. 2
    Open the existing Pearland page (/pearland-water-slide-rentals/) in ERS. Replace the content section by section, swapping League City references for Pearland. Rewrite each section — do not copy-paste.
  3. 3
    Set the H1 to: "Water Slide Rentals in Pearland, TX" — this is also the H1 fix for Pearland from Bucket 2 (Task 5), so this page is done on that front.
  4. 4
    Write a 100–150 word intro paragraph that includes: Pearland as the service city, PKA's address on Shadow Creek Pkwy, neighborhoods served (Silverlake, Shadow Creek Ranch, Pomona, Meridiana), and at least one natural use of "water slide rentals Pearland."
  5. 5
    Add a service radius note — "We deliver to all of Pearland, Manvel, Rosharon, Iowa Colony, and surrounding Brazoria County." This establishes geographic clustering and helps nearby pages rank.
  6. 6
    Add 3–5 water slide products to the page (same products on the League City page are fine). Confirm each product links to its individual item page.
  7. 7
    Add internal links FROM this page to nearby priority city pages: Iowa Colony, Manvel, Rosharon, and Sienna. This is part of the orphaned page fix (Task 6) and should be done here at the same time. Use anchor text like "Water Slide Rentals in Manvel" linking to /water-slides-rentals-manvel/.
  8. 8
    Add internal links TO this page from: (a) the /category/water-slides/ page in a "Cities We Serve" or "Popular Cities" section, and (b) the homepage service area section or footer.
  9. 9
    Set the page title tag and meta description — see Task 4 for the exact formats.
  10. 10
    Publish and submit the URL for indexing in Google Search Console: GSC → URL Inspection → paste https://www.partykidsamerica.com/pearland-water-slide-rentals/ → Request Indexing.
Suggested Intro Paragraph
Planning a backyard party in Pearland? Party Kids America delivers water slide rentals directly to your door — from our location on Shadow Creek Pkwy, we serve all of Pearland, Silverlake, Shadow Creek Ranch, Pomona, Meridiana, and surrounding areas. Choose from towering dual-lane slides and splash combos, all with free delivery, setup, and breakdown included.

Recover the Sugar Land Page

Due May 25 ERS

Sugar Land is the highest search-demand city in the entire priority list — 157 impressions in April, the most of any location page. It was near the top of page 1 in March (position 12.7) and fell 11+ positions to 24.3 in April. This is the most urgent page to stabilize because the traffic was already there and is actively being lost.

Steps

  1. 1
    In GSC, go to Performance → Pages and filter for /water-slides-rentals-sugarland/. Set the date range to the last 90 days and look at the position chart by date. Identify the exact week the drop started — this tells you whether it was a content change, a competitor move, or a Google algorithm update.
  2. 2
    In an incognito browser, search "water slide rentals Sugar Land TX" and note the top 3 organic results. Open each and do a quick content audit: How long is their intro? Do they have an FAQ? Service area list? Reviews on page? This tells you what the page needs to be competitive.
  3. 3
    Open /water-slides-rentals-sugarland/ in ERS. Check for an H1 tag. If missing, add: "Water Slide Rentals in Sugar Land, TX" — this covers the H1 fix for this page from Task 5.
  4. 4
    Compare the current page word count against the League City page. If Sugar Land is significantly shorter, expand the content: add a neighborhood section (First Colony, Greatwood, Riverstone, New Territory), a service radius note mentioning Missouri City and Stafford, and a "what's included" section if not already present.
  5. 5
    Add internal links TO this page from: the category page (/category/water-slides/), the homepage service area section, and the Missouri City page (/water-slides-rentals-missouri-city/). Sugar Land and Missouri City are geographically close — each should link to the other.
  6. 6
    Add internal links FROM this page to Missouri City and Friendswood — the two geographically closest priority pages. This contributes to the orphaned page fix from Task 6.
  7. 7
    Set title tag and meta description — see Task 4 for the exact Sugar Land copy.
  8. 8
    Publish changes and submit for GSC recrawl: URL Inspection → https://www.partykidsamerica.com/water-slides-rentals-sugarland/ → Request Indexing.
Why this is urgent: Sugar Land's drop from position 12.7 to 24.3 in one month is the steepest decline of any location page. At 157 impressions per month it has the most demand. Every week it stays at position 24 is bookings going to a competitor. This needs to be live by May 25.

GBP — Water Slide Service Optimization

Due May 20 Google Business Profile

"Near me" queries are heavily weighted by GBP signals. Adding water slides explicitly to the services list, uploading location-tagged photos, and confirming service cities will directly improve PKA's visibility for "water slide rentals near me" across Pearland and the priority service area. GBP changes typically show ranking impact within 1–2 weeks — the fastest lever available.

Steps

  1. 1
    Log in to business.google.com and open the Party Kids America profile.
  2. 2
    Go to Edit profile → Services. Find or create a "Water Slides" service category. Add individual services: "Water Slide Rentals," "Dual Lane Water Slide Rental," "Inflatable Water Slide Rental," "Backyard Water Slide Rental." For each, write a 1–2 sentence description mentioning the city (Pearland, Houston area, SE Texas).
  3. 3
    Go to Edit profile → Service area. Confirm all of the following are listed: Pearland, Iowa Colony, Manvel, Friendswood, Missouri City, Sugar Land, South Houston, Rosharon, League City, Alvin, Sienna, Shadow Creek Ranch. Add any missing. Do not add Fresno at this time.
  4. 4
    Go to Add photos. Upload 5–10 photos of actual water slide setups. Before uploading, use GeoImgr to embed GPS coordinates for Pearland (lat: 29.5636, lng: -95.3613) into the EXIF data. This tells Google the photos are from the Pearland service area.
  5. 5
    In the photo descriptions, write: "Water slide rental delivered to a backyard party in Pearland, TX — Party Kids America." Include the city name in every photo description.
  6. 6
    Go to Edit profile → Description. Audit the current description. If "water slide rentals" isn't mentioned explicitly, add it. Make sure "Pearland" and "Southeast Houston" both appear in the description.
  7. 7
    Create a GBP Post: 100–150 words promoting summer water slide rentals. Include "water slide rentals in Pearland," a photo of a setup, and a Book Now button linking to /pearland-water-slide-rentals/. Posts signal recency and keep the profile active.
Timing: GBP updates typically reflect in local rankings within 1–2 weeks. Complete by May 20 so the signal has time to compound before June.

Rewrite Title Tags + Meta Descriptions

Due May 20 ERS

Title tags and meta descriptions are the first thing a searcher sees in Google results. Weak titles suppress CTR even when a page ranks — creating a negative feedback loop. Update these across all waterslide pages. Title tag changes improve CTR within days of being recrawled.

Steps

  1. 1
    In ERS, open each waterslide page listed below. Go to Page Settings → SEO (or equivalent meta fields). Update the Title and Meta Description using the formats below.
  2. 2
    Keep title tags under 60 characters. Keep meta descriptions between 140–155 characters. Verify with charactercountonline.com before saving.
  3. 3
    After saving, verify each page in GSC URL Inspection to confirm Google picks up the new tags within 48–72 hours.

H1 Tags — All Waterslide Location Pages

Due June 1 ERS

The technical audit found 61 pages across the site missing H1 tags. For waterslide location pages specifically, a missing H1 means Google has no clear on-page signal for what city or service the page targets. Pearland (Task 1) and Sugar Land (Task 2) get their H1s as part of those rebuilds. This task covers every other waterslide location page.

Steps

  1. 1
    Open each waterslide location page in ERS. In the page editor, look for an H1 field or the first heading block. If the page builder uses visual blocks, switch to the HTML/source view to confirm what tag the heading is actually using — a large styled text block may not be a true H1.
  2. 2
    Work through this checklist in order (Pearland and Sugar Land are already handled in Tasks 1 and 2):
    • /water-slides-rentals-manvel/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Manvel, TX"
    • /water-slides-rentals-iowa-colony/ (or similar slug) — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Iowa Colony, TX"
    • /water-slides-rentals-friendswood/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Friendswood, TX"
    • /water-slides-rentals-missouri-city/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Missouri City, TX"
    • /houston-water-slide-rentals/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in South Houston, TX"
    • /water-slide-rentals-rosharon/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Rosharon, TX"
    • /water-slide-rentals-sienna/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Sienna, TX"
    • /water-slides-rentals-alvin/ — H1: "Water Slide Rentals in Alvin, TX"
    • /water-slides-rentals-league-city/ — verify H1 exists (this page is ranking well, so it likely does)
    • /category/water-slides/ — verify or update H1: "Water Slide Rentals in SE Houston, TX"
  3. 3
    Confirm no page has multiple H1 tags. The audit flagged this as a known issue on some pages. In ERS, check that each page has exactly one H1 — any additional headings should be H2 or H3.
  4. 4
    Document which pages had H1s added — keep a running list for the completion report to Kirk.
Format rule: All H1s follow the same pattern — "Water Slide Rentals in [City], TX." Do not add the brand name to the H1 (that goes in the title tag). Keep it clean and keyword-focused.
6

Fix Orphaned Waterslide Pages — Internal Linking

Due June 1 ERS + GSC

The technical audit found 79 pages across the site with no internal links pointing to them — Google struggles to find and consistently index these. Several waterslide location pages are in this group. Note: not all 79 are waterslide pages — this task focuses specifically on the waterslide and priority city pages within that set. A site-wide pass for all 79 pages is a separate project.

Step 1 — Identify Which Waterslide Pages Are Orphaned

  1. 1
    In GSC, go to Links → Internal links. Sort by "Links to page" count ascending. Any waterslide location page showing 0 or 1 internal links is effectively orphaned. Make a list.
  2. 2
    Cross-reference that list against the priority city order: Iowa Colony, Manvel, Friendswood, Missouri City, Sugar Land, South Houston. These get fixed first.

Step 2 — Add Internal Links to Orphaned Pages

  1. 1
    Category page fix (highest leverage): Open /category/water-slides/ in ERS. Add a "Cities We Deliver To" or "Water Slide Rentals Near You" section with a link to every waterslide location page. Use descriptive anchor text: "Water Slide Rentals in Manvel," "Water Slide Rentals in Iowa Colony," etc. This single edit passes internal link equity to all location pages simultaneously and is the most efficient fix available.
  2. 2
    Pearland page links (done in Task 1): The Pearland rebuild (Task 1, Step 7) adds links from Pearland to Iowa Colony, Manvel, Rosharon, and Sienna. Confirm these were added and mark them complete here.
  3. 3
    Homepage check: Open the PKA homepage. If there is a service area section, city list, or footer city links, confirm that the priority waterslide location pages (Pearland, Iowa Colony, Manvel, Friendswood, Missouri City, Sugar Land) are linked. Add any missing. Footer links count — they don't need to be prominent.
  4. 4
    Cross-links between nearby pages: Each location page should link to 2–3 geographically close neighbors.
    • Manvel → Pearland, Rosharon, Iowa Colony
    • Iowa Colony → Pearland, Manvel, Alvin
    • Friendswood → League City, Pearland
    • Missouri City → Sugar Land, South Houston
    • Sugar Land → Missouri City, Friendswood (done in Task 2)
  5. 5
    Sitemap check: Go to GSC → Sitemaps. Confirm the sitemap is submitted and all waterslide location page URLs are included. If any are missing, add them to the sitemap and resubmit. This is especially important for Iowa Colony if the page was built recently.

Step 3 — Verify in GSC

  1. 1
    After implementing, use GSC URL Inspection on each previously orphaned page. Confirm the page is indexed and the "Referring pages" section is showing internal links. If a page still shows 0 referring pages after 1 week, request indexing manually.
Why this works for rankings: Internal links pass PageRank. A waterslide page with zero internal links pointing to it cannot accumulate authority no matter how good the content is. Adding the category page "Cities We Deliver To" section alone is the single highest-ROI task in this entire bucket.
