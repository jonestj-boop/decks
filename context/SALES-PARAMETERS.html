Pitch Content Guardrails

5A. Approved Claims & Language

You MAY say
  • "AI-powered content engine"
  • "Strategy-led, data-driven"
  • "One team managing [services]"
  • "ClusterMagic content"
  • "Answer Engine Optimization" / "AEO"
  • Trust logos: Biohm, Breyers, Char-Broil, Cloudflare, Dove Men+Care, Fruit of the Loom, Harvard, Jobcase, Staples, Techstars, Unilever, Y Combinator
You MUST NOT say
  • "Guaranteed results" or performance guarantees — exception: guaranteed maps pack ranking (top 3 within 90 days) is approved for Local SEO + GMR packages only (Section 1B)
  • Specific ROI numbers without "directional, not guarantees" disclaimer
  • "We'll get you to #1" or organic ranking guarantees
  • Claims about services not in the engagement
  • Unverified case studies or results

5B. ROI Projections

  • Must include: "These are directional projections, not guarantees."
  • Base on verifiable data (GSC impressions, SEMrush traffic values, conversion rates)
  • Never project revenue/leads without qualifying assumptions
  • Use ranges, not point estimates ($75K–$100K, not $87K)

5C. Competitive Positioning

  • Never name competitors negatively
  • May reference competitive gaps from research
  • Differentiators: AI-powered execution speed, ClusterMagic content volume, AEO/Reddit-first strategy

Pitch Assembly Checklist

Before any pitch is sent to a prospect, verify:

All services are from the approved menu (Sections 1A–1D)
No restricted services included without Josh and/or Deanna's involvement (Section 2)
Pricing is at or above approved floors (Section 3)
Any discounts follow approved mechanisms (Section 3B)
Ad spend is a separate line item — never bundled
No performance guarantees stated (Section 5A)
ROI projections include required disclaimer (Section 5B)
Every deliverable matches its definition (Section 6)
Engagement structure matches an approved model (Section 4A)
Pricing is at or above published sell-price floors for each service

Bespoke Engagement Process

When a prospect needs something outside the standard menu:

1
Identify
Sales team identifies non-standard needs during discovery.
2
Document
Capture: prospect name, industry, requested services, budget signals, timeline, urgency.
3
Route
Flag as bespoke and hand off to Josh and/or Deanna for scoping and margin review.
4
Scope & Price
Josh/Deanna scopes custom components and confirms margins are acceptable.
5
Deliver
Sales team presents the pitch with Josh/Deanna's approved scope and pricing.
Hard Rule
Never present a bespoke pitch to a client before Josh and/or Deanna has reviewed the final scope and pricing.
